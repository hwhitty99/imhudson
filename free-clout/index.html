MyGesture
Android
I adjust the volume and screen brightness of my phone by swiping up and down on the very right edge of my screen. It's one of my favorite "Andoird tricks" to show people and I've gotten so used to it that I wouldn't want to live without it.

I've tried a handful of budgeting softwares over the years and this is the only one that's stuck. It's easy to start using, but can get really complicated if you want it to. YouTube is your best friend.
